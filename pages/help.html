Playcade Help

This page is in the form of an FAQ. If your question is not answered here, contact a Staff Member.

 

How do I join a game?

You can open the book in your hotbar (named GAMES (Right Click)) and click on the name of the server you want to join. Each item includes a description on the game and how to join.
 

How do I apply for staff?

Staff Members are typically handpicked by the Owner. They are usually experienced players who have purchased a Premium Rank on the store. Whenever they're open, you will see so in this thread, and you can submit your application here.
 

How do I message someone?

You can send someone a private message on the server by doing /msg (player) (message). You can send someone a private message on the website by clicking the envelope icon in the upper left corner of this page.
 

How do I report someone?

You can report someone by clicking on the Report page on the menu above, or clicking here.
